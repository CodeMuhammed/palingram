About Us

Palingram is a collaborative blogging platform where writers publish or feature interesting contents and get paid based on viewer-ship or unique impression.

Why we started palingram

Started in 2013, palingram is a conscious effort to help small scale bloggers and content providers to connect, create and share their stories on the same platform, with the vision of creating an ocean of knowledge that is beneficial to the general audience. palingram hopes to reveal a lot of mini blogs out there with top quality contents that are not well heard by giving them the platform to feature their stories where millions of people can benefit and appreciate them more.
